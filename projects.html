Weather Station Module

Environmental variables such as Temperature, Pressure and Humidity can be Monitered using a combination of Microcontrollers and Sensors which are Easy to use and Mobile and also Cheapper than Normal Weather Station. Arduino Mega 2560, DHT11, BMP 180 are the main Components used in this Project.

The microcontroller based weather reporting system using Arduino supporting controllers is fully automated and efficient. It does not require any manual labor or attention. We can plan and visit the places anytime we like with prior notification of the weather conditions. We can simply get the status of the weather condition and the air quality, etc. Therefore, with the help of embedded devices and sensors, any environment can be converted to a smart environment for accumulating the data and analyzing the environment with real-time monitoring. Hence, with such advances on microcontroller based , organizations are focusing on understanding the impact of weather on their operations and finding cutting-edge analytics on how to control the impact of their business.

Carbon Nanotubes using Radar System

To make an aircraft stereotype which is not detectable in sensitive arduino radar thus making it efficient for stealth and surveillance purposes. The accuracy of ultrasonic sensors used in arduino radar is 300khz to 3 mhz. Its range is 21.2 cm.

We have designed an object with its length more than that of a sensor. We have coated it with CNT and when placed in its range, the radar doesn't detect it and this application can be used in future for stealth and security purposes. Hence an object undetectable in radar frequency can be attained by CNT technology. CNT not only has EM waves absorption/scattering property but also it makes aircrafts sinewy and lighter thus improving its propulsion system. Overall it makes the aircraft economical and cost effective thus stretching a gallon of gas.

However, it is important to note that the use of radar systems for CNT detection is still an active area of research, and there may be limitations and challenges to overcome in order to fully utilize this technology.
